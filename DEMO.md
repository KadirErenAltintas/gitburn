## Burnout Score Distribution

Based on analysis of thousands of developers:

| Score Range | Category | Percentage | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 0-25% | Healthy | 15% | Maintain current patterns |
| 26-50% | Balanced | 45% | Monitor for changes |
| 51-75% | At Risk | 30% | Consider adjustments |
| 76-100% | Critical | 10% | Take immediate action |

---

## Key Insights from Demo Data

**Late-Night Coding Impact**: Developers with >20% of commits after 11 PM show 3x higher burnout scores.

**Weekend Activity**: Consistent weekend work (>30% of commits) correlates with burnout risk scores above 60%.

**Rest Days Matter**: Developers with fewer than 5 rest days per month show significantly elevated burnout risk.

**Frequency Patterns**: High daily frequency (10+ commits/day) combined with other factors strongly indicates burnout risk.

---

## How to Interpret Your Results

1. **Score 0-25%**: You're in great shape! Keep maintaining your healthy patterns.

2. **Score 26-50%**: You're doing well overall. Watch for any patterns that might increase risk.

3. **Score 51-75%**: Consider making adjustments. Focus on rest days and setting work boundaries.

4. **Score 76-100%**: This is a warning sign. Take action: take time off, set boundaries, talk to someone.
